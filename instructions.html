SIMON GAME

HOW TO PLAY

• Simon will give the first signal. (One of the Color Lenses will light up, accompanied by a tone)

• Repeat that signal by touching the same Color Lens.

• Simon will add one more signal.

• Repeat the complete secuence of signals in the same order FROM THE BEGINNING.

• Simon will keep adding signals by one. Continue to repeat Simon for as long as you can!
